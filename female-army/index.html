only female army will save the world

2031 world is ruled by corporations. In a race for political influence and profit, largest multinational corporation launches a secret project to develop virus that can create a super soldier.
After hundreds of failed tests, first successful result was a girl named Emily, whose performance exceeded all expectations. In an attempt to repeat her success, a virus was synthesized on the basis of her DNA and injected into a man, but everything went wrong. Virus mutated inside his body, changed his dna and processes in the brain, turning him into a living dead person.
This was the beginning of a global catastrophe.
At the same time, management of corporation decides to clone Emily from her DNA to create an army of soldiers. But in the cloning department, a suspicious emergency occurs and all employees die.
After 2 years, a random group of survivors finds the laboratory.
After running the equipment, they found 7,000 clone girls in life support devices.
Now, since they left laboratory, the main task of the clones is to protect humanity and fight the virus.
